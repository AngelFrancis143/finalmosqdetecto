📱 Initial Setup Instructions

1

Download the APK

Scan the QR code or click the download link above. Before installing, ensure your mobile device allows permissions to install apps from unknown sources in your security settings.

2

Install & Enable Location

Open the downloaded APK file and install the application. Once installed, open the app and navigate to settings to enable location services (GPS). This allows the app to track and geotagging your reports accurately.

3

Start Using MosqDetecto

You're all set! The app is now ready to use. Begin scanning for mosquito detection and help protect your community from mosquito-borne diseases.

🔄 Application Workflow Phases

1

User Reporting and Image Capture

  • Enable GPS/Location Services - Activate location tracking on your device
  • Capture Images - Take pictures of potential mosquito breeding grounds
  • Identify suspicious areas in your community
2

AI-Based Image Analysis

  • Intelligent Scanning - AI analyzes photos for breeding indicators
  • Detection Results - System identifies environmental risk factors
  • Automated environmental assessment of captured areas
3

Data Processing and Geotagging

  • Organized Storage - Reports organized by Barangay, Sitio, or Zone
  • Location Tagging - Each entry geotagged with GPS coordinates
  • Digital map interface integration for localized monitoring
4

Community Mapping and Aggregation

  • Area Marking - System sets a tag for detected breeding grounds
  • Community Visibility - Marks visible to other community members
  • Collaborative community awareness and response
5

Risk Classification and Alerts

  • Risk Levels - Low, Moderate, or High risk classification
  • Automated Recommendations - Prevention and cleaning suggestions
  • Based on frequency and severity of reports in each area
6

QR Code Integration for Accessibility

  • Quick Access - Scan QR code to access the website
  • Easy Distribution - Share via posters and materials
  • Convenient access for community members and stakeholders
